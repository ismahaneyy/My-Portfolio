GAwa

Frontend Developer

GAwa is a collaborative food-sharing app that revolutionizes how people order fast food. Instead of buying a whole pizza or bucket of chicken, users can pay only for the slices or portions they want. When enough people join the same order, the full meal is purchased and everyone gets their share. As the frontend developer, I focused on creating an intuitive user interface that makes food sharing seamless and engaging. The app promotes community building while reducing food waste and costs for users.
